About

Jabor Memorial Park is conveniently located in Kuantan, the state capital of Pahang. View map
Wholly-owned by Hock Thai Heng Caskets Trading and approved by the Pahang State Government,
it is the only and largest memorial columbarium in the state. The park is designed and developed on
the garden and landscaped memorial park concept and professionally managed. To meet the needs
of the modern society and the growing shortage and escalating cost of burial ground, the park has
a 7-story fully air-conditioned Feng Shui Columbarium built at the highest point of the park. The
site of this elegant and imposing pavilion has been carefully selected with excellent geomancy.
Niches with different directions and positions are available at the Columbarium for the choice of
clients. Separate sections are provided for the Christian and other faiths.
Prior to the development of the Jabor Memorial Park, several renowned Feng Shui masters, or
geomancists from China, Singapore, Hong Kong, Taiwan and Malaysia were specially retained to
survey the site and they agreed that the site is an ideal resting place with excellent geomancy
merits both for the deceased and their descendants.
