Ashes Compartment


For thousands of years until now, filial piety has been the very essence of the Chinese culture and traditions. Because of this fine tradition, when parents pass away, to repay the profound kindness of their parents, children will look for an ideal and pleasant eternal resting place where they can have memorial rites and offer sacrifices to the deceased in a pleasant and clean ambience, as well as for the perpetual money of their loved ones. In recent years, privately developed memorial parks have mushroomed in the country and are fast gaining popularity among Malaysians, especially the Chinese community.
