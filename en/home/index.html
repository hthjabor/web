Hock Thay Hing trading funeral services was establishehed in 1972, by the founder Mr.Lee Law.
In 1993, the company had begun cremation and memorial park services in Jabor.
Hock Thay Hing trading is one of the Malaysia's leading funeral services.

For thousands of years until now, filial piety has been the very essence of the Chinese culture and traditions. Because of this fine tradition, when parents pass away, to repay the profound kindness of their parents, children will look for an ideal and pleasant eternal resting place where they can have memorial rites and offer sacrifices to the deceased in a pleasant and clean ambience, as well as for the perpetual money of their loved ones. In recent years, privately developed memorial parks have mushroomed in the country and are fast gaining popularity among Malaysians, especially the Chinese community.A Nursing Home is constructed in the park to provide professional nursing care for the sick. The Jabor Memorial Park is fully equipped with a wide range of services and facilities. These include an environment-friendly crematory which confirms to high health standards; spacious and clean funeral halls for religious rites and keeping night vigil to allow relatives and friends to pay their respects to the deceased in a neat, solemn setting. In addition, high monks will be invited to conduct Buddhist rites during the All Ghosts Festival and sutra chanting ceremonies will be held for the benefit of the deceased and their descendants.
